Welcome to Books Before Barriers

Breaking down barriers to literacy, one book at a time.

Our Mission

To foster a love of reading and learning among underserved children by providing them with access to a diverse range of books and educational resources. We believe that literacy is a fundamental right and a key to unlocking a child's full potential. By breaking down the economic and social barriers to book ownership, we aim to create a more equitable world where every child has the opportunity to succeed.

Our Impact

Since our founding, we have distributed over 10,000 books to children in 5 communities, partnered with 12 local schools, and inspired countless young readers. Our programs have shown a measurable increase in literacy rates and classroom engagement.

Our Goals

  • Expand our reach to 10 new communities within the next two years.
  • Launch a digital literacy program to complement our book distribution efforts.
  • Establish a mentorship program connecting volunteers with young readers.
  • Secure long-term funding to ensure the sustainability of our mission.
